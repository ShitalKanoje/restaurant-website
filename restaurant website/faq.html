THE SPICE ROOM KITCHEN
FAQ



Questions about The Spice Room Kitchen| Check out our FAQ’s below. Didn’t see an answer? We’re here to help. Just send us an email: TheSpiceRoomKitchen.com.



DO YOU HAVE HIGHCHAIRS?
Yes, we have both highchairs and booster seats.

CAN YOU ACCOMMODATE STROLLERS AND WHEELCHAIRS?
Yes.

DO YOU OFFER VEGETARIAN/VEGAN/GLUTEN FREE DISHES?
We do! We have a quite a few options for each just ask your server or check out our menu!

DO YOU HAVE OUTDOOR SEATING?
We do have outdoor seating! We have bigger one at Colfax location.

DO YOU HAVE TAKEOUT/DELIVERY?
Yes! To place an order for pickup or delivery click here.

ARE PETS ALLOWED?
Service dogs are always welcome inside or out, otherwise well-behaved dogs are welcome on our front patio!

DO YOU TAKE RESERVATIONS?
We do accept reservations. Please make online reservations for parties of 1 to 6. If there is no availability showing,
please put yourself on our waitlist or contact the restaurant directly as we may be able to assist.
Parties of seven or larger groups please call us to make reservations. We look forward to welcoming you soon.

ARE RESERVATIONS REQUIRED?
We strongly recommend that our guests reserve a table so there is no disappointment upon arrival at having to wait.
You can reserve a table by clicking here . But reservations are not required. We will be delighted to accommodate you if we are able.
